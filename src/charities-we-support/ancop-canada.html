ANCOP International Canada, Inc. is a duly registered Canadian charity dedicated to uplift the lives of poor families in developing countries through shelter, child education, and humanitarian relief.

In 2014, The Tessa Beauchamp Foundation began sponsoring three children from the village of Avanai in the Philippines by providing funds to ANCOP. The children will be provided with a better education and will receive ongoing healthcare.
